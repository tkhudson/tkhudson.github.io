# Privacy Policy for Dig 4 Dinos

**Last Updated:** December 10, 2025
**Effective Date:** December 10, 2025

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any user data.**

Dig 4 Dinos operates entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or device information to external servers.

## Local Storage Only

The app saves your game progress (unlocked tools, collected bones, and settings) locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

## No Third-Party Services

The app does not use:

- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services
- Crash reporting services

## Children's Privacy

This app is safe for children of all ages. Since we collect no data whatsoever, there are no privacy concerns for users under 13 years old.

## Data Deletion

To delete all game data, simply uninstall the app or clear the app's data through your device settings.
